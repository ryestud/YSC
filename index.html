Empowering children joining the world of STEM while providing inclusivity, opportunity, and big steps towards a bright future.

Our Mission

Opening the minds and hearts of K through 12 children and their families to the world of STEM. Using a unique Mastery learning program integrating math, chess, coding, financial support and mentoring to instill the qualities that will lead to success in life.

Our Services

Tutoring


Students walk through our long-term, End-To-End appraoch. We take students with little-to-no foundation in STEM and coach them our process that builds a strong foundation in critical & creative thinking through...

  • Chess

  • Math

  • Computer Science (Python, Linux, Java)

Personal Development


Students learn social and teamwork skills that will help them in all areas of their lives. They will learn through...


  • Group work

  • Individual Mentoring

  • Competitions

...among other things and be able to apply their newfound personal skills to all other areas of their lives.

College Preparedness


Students have access to financial aid and college application support to better prepare them for their future.


  • Mentors will sit down with students and help them with resumes and college applications

  • Financial aid opportunities will be provided

  • Chess and STEM competitions will help students and boost their resumes
